# Strategic Recommendations for HealthStat

## 1. Operational Improvements
- Streamline discharge planning to reduce LOS
- Introduce early rounding and care coordination teams
- Implement patient-flow management systems
- Improve diagnostic turnaround times

## 2. Financial Optimization
- Investigate cost outliers in low-severity cases
- Review staffing levels relative to patient volume
- Optimize inventory management and procurement
- Standardize treatment pathways to reduce unnecessary variability

## 3. Quality & Care Enhancements
- Expand capacity for severe/extreme severity cases
- Strengthen triage efficiency
- Enhance nursing and specialty care training

## 4. Technology & Data Strategy
- Introduce predictive LOS models
- Automate monthly and quarterly performance reports
- Integrate satisfaction and readmission metrics
- Develop real-time capacity dashboards for operational control
